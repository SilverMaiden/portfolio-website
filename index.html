Social Justice - Maven Youth

Maven Youth works alongside youth and tech professionals to ensure the transfer of intersectionality and cross generational tech inclusion. After an incred­i­ble expe­ri­ence at the 2015 Maven Youth tech camp in SF con­vinced me to become a soft­ware engi­neer, I resolved to do my part in help­ing the pro­gram to grow and have spent the last 5 years working with the CEO to provide LGBT+ youth across the country with tech opportunities and education.
